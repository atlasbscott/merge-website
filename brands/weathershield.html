About the Brand

Weather-Tested, Architect-Approved

WeatherShield has built its reputation on one principle: windows should stand up to whatever nature delivers. Their wood and aluminum-clad products are engineered with proprietary weather-resistant technologies, including their ProShield finish system that guards against moisture, UV exposure, and temperature extremes.

The product line includes casement, double-hung, awning, picture, and specialty shape configurations, all available in wood or clad construction. WeatherShield's Visions series offers a contemporary aesthetic with clean profiles, while their Legacy series caters to traditional and historic architectural styles with detailed muntin patterns and classic proportions.

Merge recommends WeatherShield for projects where long-term durability is a top priority. Our team can help you navigate the product line, compare finish options, and select the right glazing package for Texas energy codes. Visit our showroom to see WeatherShield units in person.

Why WeatherShield

Protection Built In

ProShield Finish

WeatherShield's proprietary ProShield finish system provides a factory-applied protective coating that resists moisture, UV rays, and temperature swings far beyond standard paint or stain.

Tested to withstand extreme wind loads and water infiltration pressures, WeatherShield products meet the strictest performance ratings in the industry.

Design Versatility
