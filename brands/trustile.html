About the Brand

Interior Doors That Define the Space

TruStile builds interior doors for projects where the details matter. Their catalog spans hundreds of panel configurations, from traditional raised-panel and Shaker profiles to flush doors with clean, modern lines. Every door is made to order in Denver, Colorado, using MDF, wood veneer, or paint-grade materials depending on the design intent and budget.

What sets TruStile apart is the depth of their customization. Architects can specify exact stile and rail widths, panel profiles, glass inserts, and even integrated barn door hardware. Their modern collection includes zero-stile flush doors and pivot doors that read as architectural elements rather than standard interior millwork. For builders working on high-end custom homes, TruStile provides the interior door package that matches the level of everything else in the project.

Merge is your TruStile specification partner in DFW. Our team helps design teams select door styles, coordinate finishes across the full interior door package, and manage the ordering process so that every unit arrives on schedule and ready to hang.
