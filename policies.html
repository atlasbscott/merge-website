Return Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Because the majority of our products are custom-ordered to your specifications, returns are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please review the following guidelines.

Custom Orders

Custom-manufactured windows, doors, and related products are non-returnable and non-refundable once the order has been placed with the manufacturer. We strongly encourage you to verify all specifications, dimensions, and finishes before approving your order. Our team reviews every order in detail before submission to minimize errors.

Stock Items

In-stock or standard items may be eligible for return within 30 days of delivery, provided they are in original, unopened packaging and free of damage. A restocking fee of up to 25% may apply. Return shipping is the responsibility of the buyer.

Damaged or Defective Products

If a product arrives damaged or defective, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will coordinate with the manufacturer to arrange a replacement or repair under warranty. Please document any damage with photographs at the time of delivery.

Cancellations

Orders may be cancelled without penalty if the cancellation request is received before the order is submitted to the manufacturer. Once an order is in production, cancellation may not be possible or may be subject to fees determined by the manufacturer.

Questions

For any questions regarding returns, cancellations, or warranty claims, contact our service team at info@mergewd.com or (817) 600-3454.
